This             text

        spans              several
   lines

                     and             has             lots
  of         extra              

whitespace.

Above there is a sentence that stated "This text spans several lines and has lots of extra whitespace" but this separated by lots of white spaces.

Same Text in a Paragraph Element

This text spans several lines and has lots of extra whitespace.

The paragraph elements ignores the whitespaces and only puts one space between the words regardless of how many words are in the source text.
